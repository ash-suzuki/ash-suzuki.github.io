About me

Research Interests: Applied Mathematics for Machine Learning

  • Small machine learning
    by Riemannian-manifold-based (including hyperbolic-space-based) machine learning and optimization.
  • Evaluating machine learning's limitation
    by learning theory and information theory.
  • Manipulating and understanding multi-media data (e.g., images, languages, audio, etc.)
    by deep learning, geometry, and algebra.
